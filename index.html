About

Valentina is the soul behind Flowers Paradise, a woman who sees beauty in every petal and turns flowers into stories. Her passion began in childhood, when she spent hours in her grandmother's garden, fascinated by the colors, perfumes and delicacy of nature. Today, she creates arrangements that bring emotion, elegance and a touch of magic to every person's life. For Valentina, flowers are not just decorations, but a language of feelings, an art form and a joy shared with those around her.
